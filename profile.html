My name is Emmit Kamakaniʻoluʻoluʻokoʻolau Kahaʻulelio Parubrub and I am a student at the University of San Francisco majoring in computer science. I was born on the island of Oahu, Hawaiʻi and raised in Kahaluʻu. I love being in the ocean, the mountains, or the gym but I have always been fascinated with technology. When I was a child my environment was very rural and filled with nature so when I would see new technology I would be interested and intrigued by how it worked. I am majoring in computer science because I want to build the next big thing in technology that fosters that fascination and passion that I used to have as a child into others.
